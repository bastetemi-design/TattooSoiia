Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

The studio is based in Bondi Beach, NSW. Full address details are shared once your consultation is confirmed.

Is a deposit required?

Yes — a non-refundable deposit secures your booking once the design and date are confirmed. It's deducted from the final session cost.

How old do I have to be to get a tattoo?

You must be 18 or older with valid photo ID. No exceptions, regardless of parental consent.

Can I see the design before my appointment?

Yes — final artwork is shared for approval ahead of your session, with time for any small adjustments.
